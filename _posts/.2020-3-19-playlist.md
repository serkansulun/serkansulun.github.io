Made a 7 hour long playlist, with mostly psychedelic rock, classical, jazz and instrumental. I recommend not to shuffle it, because some songs follow each other. Plus, I arranged it in a way that there are abrupt changes, which is a characteristic of the playlist. It is mostly mellow and cheerful, but it can get fierce and dark, which is a part of the journey.
